**Clinic Appointment System**

The Clinic Appointment System is a web-based application designed to streamline the management of patient appointments, prescriptions, and medical records. Patients can register, book appointments, view prescriptions, download prescription PDFs, and access their medical history. Doctors can manage appointments, approve or reject requests, issue prescriptions, and maintain patient records. The system improves communication between doctors and patients while providing an organized and efficient healthcare management platform..
